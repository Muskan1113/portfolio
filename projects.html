My Portfolio Website


This project showcases my skills, experiences, and projects. It serves as a central hub to present my work and provide information about me.

Features

Home: The home section introduces visitors to my portfolio and provides a brief overview of what they can expect to find on the website.

About: The about section gives insights into my background, education, skills, and interests. It provides a deeper understanding of who I am as a professional.

Projects: The projects section highlights some of my notable projects. Each project is accompanied by a description, key features, and technologies used.

Contact: The contact section provides visitors with a means to get in touch with me. It includes a contact form and relevant contact information.

Navigation: The navigation menu at the top allows easy access to different sections of the website. Visitors can navigate between the home, about, projects, and contact pages seamlessly.

Technologies Used

HTML5: For structuring the web pages.

CSS3: For styling the website and creating an appealing design.

JavaScript: For interactivity and functionality, such as smooth scrolling and form validation.
